How to Use Scribe

Welcome to Scribe! This guide will help you navigate and make the most out of your teaching experience.

Accessing Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll land on your dashboard, providing a comprehensive view of all your assigned classes. It's your central hub for managing your teaching activities.

Starting a Lecture Recording

Initiate a lecture recording effortlessly by clicking the Microphone button on your dashboard. Ensure you select the correct class to keep your recordings organized.

Recording a Lecture

Once you've selected a class, you'll be directed to the Recording Page. Enter the Lecture Title and hit Record. The system will automatically transcribe your lecture, making it easy to focus on teaching.

Finishing and Reviewing the Lecture

Conclude your lecture by pressing the Stop button. Review the transcribed text and make any necessary edits to ensure accuracy and clarity.

Saving and Uploading the Lecture

After finalizing your transcript, save and upload the lecture notes to the class page. This allows students to access the materials at their convenience, enhancing their learning experience.

Managing Your Lectures

Revisit and update your lectures anytime through your dashboard. This flexibility ensures your content remains current and relevant.
