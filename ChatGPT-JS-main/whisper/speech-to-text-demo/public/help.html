How to Use Scribe

Welcome to Scribe! This guide will help you navigate and make the most out of your learning experience.

Student Dashboard

After logging in, you will arrive at your Dashboard where you can:

  • View all your enrolled courses at a glance
  • Search for specific courses using the search bar
  • Sort your courses by name or date added

Viewing Courses and Lectures

Accessing your course content is simple:

  1. Click on a course from your dashboard
  2. You will see a list of lectures conducted for that course
  3. Select a lecture to view its content

Viewing and Modifying Lecture Notes

When viewing a lecture, you will see:

  • Formatted Lecture Notes - Generated from the face-to-face discussion using speech-to-text technology
  • My Notes - A section where you can add your personal notes while studying

Using the Modify Notes Feature

To customize how you view your notes:

  1. On the lecture page, find the three-dot button ()
  2. Click this button to open additional options
  3. Select one or multiple options depending on how you want to study
Option Description
Summarization Generate a concise summary of the lecture content to quickly review the main points
Flashcards Create study flashcards from key concepts to help reinforce important topics
Audio Access the lecture recording player to listen to the original lecture anytime
Visual Learning View relevant images automatically sourced from key lecture concepts to better understand the material
Quizzes Test your knowledge and track your progress with interactive questions based on the lecture content

If you have any questions or need additional help, please contact your instructor or the Scribe support team.
